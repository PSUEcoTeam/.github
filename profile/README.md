## About Us

**PSU Eco Team** is the official competitive engineering team of **Prince Sultan University (PSU)**, Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. We design, build, and race ultra-efficient and autonomous vehicles in the **Shell Eco-Marathon** — the world's most rigorous energy efficiency competition.

We are the **#1-ranked Shell Eco-Marathon team in the Middle East**, with consecutive top finishes since 2022, and top rankings across **Asia Pacific**. In 2026, we make history as the **first Saudi team to compete in the Autonomous category** at Shell Eco-Marathon **Europe & Africa**, held at the Silesia Ring, Poland.

We don't just build cars. We build engineers.

## 2026 Autonomous Mission

> **First Saudi team. Autonomous category. Shell Eco-Marathon Europe & Africa. Poland.**

Our 2026 system is a full-stack autonomous driving platform engineered under strict energy constraints:

```
Perception Layer   →   Intel RealSense D435i + ZED 2 | YOLOv8 | OpenCV
Planning Layer     →   Path planning + obstacle avoidance | ROS 2
Control Layer      →   Drive-by-wire (throttle, steering, braking)
Compute Platform   →   NVIDIA Jetson Orin NX
Communication      →   MQTT telemetry | CAN bus | real-time dashboards
Energy Budget      →   Full autonomous operation within Shell SEM limits
```

Every subsystem is designed to win — not just complete.
